at Stockholm University

In Sweden the PhD studies are usually 4 years long. If the PhD student decides to teach he/she will obtain an extension of 1 year. In this case the work load will be divided in 80% for research and 20% for teaching. This consists of 300 hours of teaching duties per academic year (conted with preparation). The teaching for Bachelor courses is in Swedish, while it is in English for the Master Level.
I taught approximately 1400 hours, with duties comprehending tutoring, exercises sessions, and lecturing an entire class. I refer to my CV for a full list of my teaching duties.
